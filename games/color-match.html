Color Match

Focus

Test your attention and reaction speed by determining if the color of a word matches its meaning. This game is based on the Stroop Effect, which effectively trains your brain's ability to inhibit automatic responses.

Instructions

In this game, you will see a colored word. Your task is to determine if the color of the word matches its meaning.

  • If the color of the word matches its meaning (e.g., the word "red" displayed in red), click the "Match" button.
  • If the color of the word does not match its meaning (e.g., the word "red" displayed in blue), click the "No Match" button.
  • The game lasts for 60 seconds. Answer as quickly and accurately as possible.
  • You earn 1 point for each correct answer. Incorrect answers do not deduct points.
